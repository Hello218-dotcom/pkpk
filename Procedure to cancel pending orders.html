Procedure to Cancel Pending Orders During System Failure

If IBTS/KITS are down, investors may still manage orders through KATS desk.

What To Do If System Is Down

You can call the KATS operator to:

  1. Check lined-up orders.
  2. Cancel pending orders.
  3. Place new orders.
  4. Modify existing orders.
TIP: Keep Client ID, Last 6 digits of CNIC, Symbol, Quantity ready.
