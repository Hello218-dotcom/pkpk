Faysal (Faisal) Dawood

CEO

Faysal Dawood is a seasoned professional with over 20 years of experience in Pakistan’s capital markets. 16+ yrs investor Equity Markets Portfolio Mindset

Salman Hussain is a seasoned investor and finance professional with over 16 years of experience in Pakistan’s capital markets. He has maintained an equity trading account with AKD Securities Limited since March 25, 2008 and actively engages in portfolio management.
